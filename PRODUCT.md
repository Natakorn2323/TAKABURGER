# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Bangkok-based food seekers discovering TAKABURGER through mobile search and social. They're hungry, in motion (commuting, at work, between activities), and deciding whether to visit or order. They expect fast site load, clear menu presentation, easy ordering on-the-go, and confident brand presence that reflects authenticity and craft.

## Product Purpose

TAKABURGER is a premium, handcrafted burger restaurant telling its story through a beautiful web presence. The site showcases fresh ingredients, daily-crafted patties, and Thai-inspired flavors to drive foot traffic and online orders. Success means mobile visitors feel the brand's authenticity, find menu information instantly, and convert to either in-store visits or delivery orders.

## Brand Personality

Authentic · Crafted · Thai

Voice: Expert confidence without pretension. Proud of quality without marketing noise. Conversational, warm, grounded in craft.

## Anti-references

- Over-designed minimalism that hides the product
- Generic SaaS-style restaurant templates (hero-metric, identical cards, tiny eyebrows)
- Cluttered food photos competing for attention
- Unclear CTAs or buried ordering flow
- Responsive failures on mobile (stacked navigation, unreadable type, hidden content)

## Design Principles

1. **Craft visible** — Show ingredient quality, daily-fresh processes, and handmade care. Not generic.
2. **Mobile-first hierarchy** — Clearest path on small screens: brand story → menu → order. No cognitive overload.
3. **Thai voice, global taste** — Honor local language, cultural references, and flavors without alienating non-Thai seekers.
4. **Speed and scannability** — Fast load, fast menu scan, fast ordering. Respect mobile bandwidth and attention.
5. **Authentic visuals** — Real ingredients, real craftsmanship. No stock photography or trendy gradients that contradict "handmade."

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG 2.1 AA (contrast, tap targets, keyboard navigation)
- Thai language fully supported (no text clipping, proper line-height for script)
- Image alt text for all menu items and brand visuals
- Reduced motion respected (no auto-play video, gentle transitions)
- Mobile-first viewport optimization (no horizontal scroll)
